GOOGLE SHEETS RSVP SETUP

1. Create a Google Sheet with columns: Timestamp, Name, Attendance, Message.
2. Open Extensions > Apps Script.
3. Paste the contents of google-apps-script.js and save.
4. Deploy > New deployment > Web app.
5. Execute as: Me. Access: Anyone.
6. Copy the Web App URL.
7. Open script.js and paste it between the quotation marks in:
   const GOOGLE_SCRIPT_URL="";
8. Upload the updated folder to GitHub/Netlify.
